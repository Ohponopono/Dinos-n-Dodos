Sifter

  The Sifter is the first crafting station you will likely use in Dinos & Dodos. The Sifter is used to sift through sand, red sand and gravel to acquire fossils and other items.

(Recipe for the sifter, performed in the Crafting Table)

  To use the sifter simply place a sand, red sand or gravel on top of it, the block eventually breaks and drops an item. The time taken to sift varies depending on when the block is placed onto the sifter.

  Depending on what you sift you can get different items out of the sifter. Sifting red sand will give you fossils commonly and give you less junk items while sifting gravel will give you fossils uncommonly and give you more junk items, sand is a middle ground between the two blocks.

Analyzer

  Analyzers are used to analyze fossils, inputting a Paper and a Fossil will give a Genetic Note.

(Recipe for the Analyzer, performed in the Crafting Table)

  The GUI of the analyzer is pretty self explanatory, but for the sake of idiots I’ll explain here. Paper should be inserted into the top slot of the analyzer and the fossil should be inserted into the bottom slot of the analyzer. Both of the items inserted will be consumed and a Genetic Note will be output.

(A recipe in the analyzer)

Needle

  Needles are an item added in Dinos & Dodos that allow you to extract the DNA of mob’s. Simply crafting a needle and then smacking a mob will store the mob's DNA into the needle. Be warned, however, that killing a mob with a needle will not yield the mob’s DNA.

(Recipe for the needle, performed in the Crafting Table)

DNA Extractor

The DNA Extractor is used to extract DNA from needles and plant-items. Simply inserting a needle with DNA in it or inserting a plant item will extract it’s DNA. Plant items are consumed upon DNA extraction, however, needles are simply emptied.

(Recipe for the DNA Extractor, performed in the Crafting Table)

DNA Combinator

The DNA Combinator is used to combine DNA with accuracies lower than 100. Inserting one DNA into the top slot and another into the bottom slot will add the accuracies together. If one of the DNA has a 100% accuracy they cannot be added together.

Genome Modifier

The Genome Modifier is used to modify the genomes of DNAs. This station is a little more complex than the others so I’ll go into more detail.

(Recipe for the Genome Modifier, performed in the Crafting Table)

  The first use of the Genome Modifier is modifying DNAs using Genetic Notes. It is important to note that every mob in the game has an Identifier that is used internally by DnD to check if some things are possible. These identifiers include Mammals, Mystery, Insects, Fish, Cetacea, Mollusks, Primates, Birds and Lizards. You can probably infer what mobs belong to what groups.

  To modify a DNA using a Genetic Note both the mob DNA and the Note must have a matching Identifier. For the sake of explaining, Genetic Notes with Dinosaur Structure in them have to be inserted into the Genome Modifier alongside either Chicken or Parrot DNA.

  The Genome Modifier doesn't just modify DNA using Genetic Notes however, it can also modify the genomes of one entity with the genomes of another. Genomes refer to the attributes of an entity, since some people probably don't know what attributes are I’ll explain. Attributes are what Minecraft uses to determine the stats of a mob, like their health, speed, armor and knockback resistance.

  Inserting two DNA into the Genome Modifier will modify the bottom DNA. The top DNAs Genome will be halved and then added to the Genome of the bottom DNA. A modified DNA cannot be modified again and attempting to extract DNA from a modified entity will simply give you modified DNA.

Cultivator

  The Cultivator is used to cultivate DNA into eggs, embryos and vats. Simply inserting a milk into the bottom slot and a DNA of 100% accuracy into the top slot will output an item corresponding to the DNA.

  Items outputting can range from Eggs to Embryo Needles to Monster Vats. Eggs and Monster Vats work similarly, right click to place or hatch them. Embryo Needles however are a bit different. Embryo needles can impregnate any mob of the same Identifier. AKA if you have a Villager Embryo you can impregnate any Primate with it, not just villagers.

(Recipe for the Cultivator, performed in the Crafting Table)

Projector

  The Projector is a decoration block that's used to create holographic projections. Simply insert a DNA or Egg into the Projector and then right click it, this will make the projector face you and also update the entity shown. A Projector can be powered with redstone to lock it, this will dispense the item contained in it and make it so right clicking does not affect it.

(Recipe for the Projector, performed in the Crafting Table)
